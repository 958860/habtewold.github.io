Meet the Characters

Each chess piece is reimagined as a unique character with personality, story, and VR abilities.

Knight Commander

Swift, fearless, and unpredictable. Leads flanking maneuvers and excels in mobility-based tactics.

Queen Strategist

A powerful force, blending offense and defense. Commanding presence with long-range influence.

Rook Enforcer

A fortress on wheels. Unstoppable in straight paths and shields allies with defensive buffs.

Bishop Oracle

Mystical and precise. Controls diagonals and uses light-based vision to reveal enemy plans.

Pawn Vanguard

Loyal and underestimated. Can evolve into stronger roles when reaching enemy territory.

King Architect

The mastermind. Must be protected at all costs. Commands the board with limited but crucial influence.
